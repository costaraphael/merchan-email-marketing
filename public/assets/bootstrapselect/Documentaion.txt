Example: http://www.a1alfred.com/legend/admin/bootstrapselect.html

Include in head: <link href="assets/bootstrapselect/css/bootstrap-select.min.css" rel="stylesheet">

Include in body: <script src="assets/bootstrapselect/js/bootstrap-select.min.js"></script>

Simple Usage:

<select class="selectpicker">
    <option>Mustard</option>
    <option>Ketchup</option>
    <option>Relish</option>
</select>

To run:

$('.selectpicker').selectpicker();
