Example: http://www.a1alfred.com/legend/admin/tinymce.html

Include in body: <script src="assets/tinymce/tinymce.min.js"></script>

Basic Usage:

<textarea id="mce" rows="10"></textarea>

To run:

tinyMCE.init({
    mode : "textareas",
    selector: "#mce"
});
