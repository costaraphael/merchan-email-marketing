Example: http://www.a1alfred.com/legend/admin/selectboxit.html

Include in head: <link href="assets/selectboxIt/css/jquery.selectBoxIt.css" rel="stylesheet">

Include in body: <script src="assets/selectboxIt/js/jquery.selectBoxIt.min.js"></script>

Basic Usage:

<select class="selectpicker" id="select">
	<option>This is a good theme</option>
	<option>Don't mean to</option>
	<option>toot my own horn here</option>
</select>

To run:

$("#select").selectBoxIt();
