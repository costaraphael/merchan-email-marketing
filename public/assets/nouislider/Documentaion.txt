Example: http://www.a1alfred.com/legend/admin/nouislider.html

Include in head: <link href="assets/nouislider/css/jquery.nouislider.min.css" rel="stylesheet">

Include in body: <script src="assets/nouislider/js/jquery.nouislider.min.js"></script>

Basic Usage:

<div id="slider1"></div>

To run:

$("#slider1").noUiSlider({
	 start: [20, 80]
	,range: [0, 100]
	,connect: true
	,handles: 2
});
