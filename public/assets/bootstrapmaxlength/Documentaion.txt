Example: http://www.a1alfred.com/legend/admin/formcomponents.html

Include in body: <script src="assets/bootstrapmaxlength/js/bootstrap-maxlength.min.js"></script>

Simple Usage:

<input id="mxlenght" maxlength="25" class="form-control" type="text" />

To run:

$("#mxlenght").maxlength({
    alwaysShow: true
});
