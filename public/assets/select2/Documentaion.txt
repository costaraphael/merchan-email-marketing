Example: http://www.a1alfred.com/legend/admin/select2.html

Include in head: <link href="assets/select2/css/select2.css" rel="stylesheet">

Include in body: <script src="assets/select2/js/select2.min.js"></script>

Basic Usage:

<select id="select">
	<option value="AK">Alaska</option>
    <option value="HI">Hawaii</option>
    <option value="CA">California</option>
</select>

To run:

$("#select").select2();
