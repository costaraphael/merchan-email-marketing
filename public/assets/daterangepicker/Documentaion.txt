Example: http://www.a1alfred.com/legend/admin/datetimepicker.html

Include in head: <link href="assets/daterangepicker/css/daterangepicker-bs3.css" rel="stylesheet">

Include in body: <script src="assets/daterangepicker/js/moment.min.js"></script>
        		 <script src="assets/daterangepicker/js/daterangepicker.js"></script>

Simple Usage:

<div class="input-group form-group">
  <span class="input-group-addon"><i class="icon-calendar"></i></span>
  <input type="text" class="form-control" placeholder="Chose Dates" id="reservation">
</div>
  
To run:

$('#reservation').daterangepicker();
