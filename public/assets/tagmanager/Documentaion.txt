Example: http://www.a1alfred.com/legend/admin/tagmanager.html

Include in head: <link href="assets/typeahead/css/typeahead.js-bootstrap.css" rel="stylesheet">
        		 <link href="assets/tagmanager/css/tagmanager.css" rel="stylesheet">

Include in body: <script src="assets/typeahead/js/typeahead.min.js"></script>
        		 <script src="assets/tagmanager/js/tagmanager.js"></script>

Basic Usage:

<input type="text" name="tags" placeholder="Tags" class="form-control" id="tag-manager" />

To run:

jQuery("#tag-manager").tagsManager();

Typeahead is for the suggested dropdown
