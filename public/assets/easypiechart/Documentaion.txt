Example: http://www.a1alfred.com/legend/admin/charts3.html

Include in body: <script src="assets/easypiechart/js/jquery.easypiechart.min.js"></script>

Simple Usage:

<p id="chart" class="text-center" data-percent="47">

To run:

$('#chart').easyPieChart({
	easing: 'easeOutBounce'
});
