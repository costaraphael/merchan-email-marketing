Example: http://www.a1alfred.com/legend/admin/datetimepicker.html

Include in head: <link href="assets/datetimepicker/css/bootstrap-datetimepicker.min.css" rel="stylesheet">

Include in body: <script src="assets/datetimepicker/js/bootstrap-datetimepicker.min.js"></script>

Simple Usage:

<div class="form-group">
	<input size="16" type="text" class="form-control" value="2012-06-15 14:45" readonly id="form_datetime">
</div>

To run:

$("#form_datetime").datetimepicker({format: 'yyyy-mm-dd hh:ii'});
