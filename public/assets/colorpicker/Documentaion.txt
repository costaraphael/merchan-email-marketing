Example: http://www.a1alfred.com/legend/admin/colorpicker.html

Include in head: <link href="assets/colorpicker/css/bootstrap-colorpicker.min.css" rel="stylesheet">

Include in body: <script src="assets/colorpicker/js/bootstrap-colorpicker.min.js"></script>

Simple Usage:

<div class="form-group">
	<input type="text" class="form-control bscp" value="#8fff00" id="cp1" >
</div>
  
To run:
$('#cp1').colorpicker();
