Example: http://www.a1alfred.com/legend/admin/datetimepicker.html

Include in head: <link href="assets/clockface/css/clockface.css" rel="stylesheet">

Include in body: <script src="assets/clockface/js/clockface.js"></script>

Simple Usage:

<div class="form-group">
	<input type="text" id="t1" value="2:30 PM" data-format="hh:mm A" class="form-control">
</div>
  
To run:

$('#t1').clockface();
